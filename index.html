Math Challenge

How to Play

  • Answer 10 math questions within the time limit.
  • Choose from three answer options (A, B, C).
  • Each question has a 30-second time limit.
  • Earn 5 points per correct answer, plus a time bonus.
  • Score at least 6 correct answers to win.
  • Use hints to remove one incorrect option (once per question).
  • Skip up to 3 questions per game.
  • Use keys 1, 2, 3 or A, B, C to select answers, S to skip, Q to quit.
  • Tab to navigate, Enter/Space to select.
